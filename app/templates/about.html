About

A Massachusetts-based community dedicated to fostering positive change in America.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower residents of Massachusetts to actively participate in shaping a more equitable, sustainable, and vibrant future for America through grassroots organizing, advocacy, and community engagement.

Our Vision

Tangible change brought to our local communities and the country at large via targeted, consistent, focused acts of resistance against the current administration.

What We Do

Advocacy & Awareness

We champion local causes, raise public awareness about critical issues, and engage with policymakers to advocate for positive change.

Community Organizing

We bring people together through meetings, protests, and events to build strong networks and mobilize for action.

Education & Resources

We provide accessible information, educational materials, and resources to help our community members stay informed and engaged.

Direct Action & Projects

From raising awareness of local elections to peaceful acts of dissent, we lead and participate in initiatives that make a direct impact.

Our Core Values

Inclusivity

We welcome all members of our community, respecting diverse perspectives and backgrounds.

Transparency

We operate with openness and integrity in all our actions and communications.

Empowerment

We strive to equip individuals with the knowledge and tools to make a difference.

Collaboration

We believe in working together with other organizations and individuals for greater impact.
