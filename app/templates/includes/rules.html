Rules

  • 1. No toxicity

    Disagreement, arguing, and debate are fine. Rudeness and harassment will not be tolerated.

  • 2. No misinformation

    Controversial takes are welcomed; misinformation is not. Information which can be definitively proven wrong and which is not constructive will be removed.

  • 3. No personal information

    Publicly available information is fair game; revealing private information is forbidden.

  • 4. Be receptive

    Always consider the other viewpoint, if only to better dismantle it.
